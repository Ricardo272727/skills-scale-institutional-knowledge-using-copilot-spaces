# Onboarding & Knowledge Capture Checklist

Purpose: Reduce ramp time and ensure institutional knowledge is captured and discoverable.

## Pre-arrival
- Prepare account access and initial environment setup instructions.
- Provide an onboarding schedule (first week overview, week-by-week milestones).

## Week 1
- Intro to codebase, architecture overview, and primary tools.
- Pairing sessions with mentor or buddy for core workflows (PR process, CI, deployments).
- Review docs: roles & personas, release checklist, incident response checklist.

## 30/60/90 day milestones
- Assign a small scope project with clear acceptance criteria.
- Check documentation contributions: ask the new hire to update or add at least one doc/runbook.
- Conduct knowledge sharing and handover sessions for critical systems.

## Knowledge capture
- Maintain decision logs for architecture and policy choices.
- Link runbooks, on-call rotations, and post-incident reviews to docs/.
- Keep onboarding material up-to-date; owners review quarterly.

## Owners
- Onboarding Coordinator / Knowledge Manager: primary owner for materials.
- Mentor/Buddy: day-to-day ramp support.
- Engineering Manager / Project Manager: progress reviews.
